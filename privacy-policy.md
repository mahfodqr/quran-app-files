# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Qalon Mushaf is a Quran reading application.

The app does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

The app may store some preferences locally on the user’s device, such as the last opened page, reading mode, theme settings, bookmarks, and downloaded high-quality Quran images. This data stays on the user’s device and is not sent to us.

The app may access the internet only to download optional high-quality Quran page images if the user chooses to download them.

The app does not show ads and does not sell or share user data with third parties.

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to provide.
